== Description ==

The fastest way to get business contacts from your site. See what kind of company is browsing your site and what they are doing on it. This plugin helps easy add Popside.co tracker to your site.

== Installation ==
* Upload the Popside plugin to your site then Activate it.
* Enter Popside tracker in Popside settings page and click submit button.
* Done!
